# Tonight readiness checklist

Use this as the pre-session pass before adding more features. The goal is a stable table loop, not more surface area.

## P0: must pass before play

### Player character loop
- Create/open a character sheet.
- Add **Studded Leather** from the equipment list, use **Add & Equip**, and confirm AC changes.
- Add a shield, use **Add & Equip**, and confirm AC changes again.
- Add a sword or bow, equip it, and confirm it appears as an attack on the combat tab.
- Roll to hit, then roll damage, and confirm both use the same dice animation.
- Open Rook suggestions, dismiss one notification, clear all, restore them, and generate one advice/fact card.

### GM live-session loop
- Open Live Play Mode.
- Confirm the default layout is focused: Combat, Party, Notes, and Handouts.
- Switch one panel to Quick Dice and confirm d20/d6/d8/etc. rolls fire.
- Create a handout with a title/content and optional attachment.
- Share the handout with a selected player.
- Confirm the handout remains visible in the GM handouts panel after sharing.

### Player handout loop
- Log in as/impersonate a player who received a handout.
- Confirm unread handouts are visible.
- Open/read the handout.
- Save it as a clue.
- Share it with another eligible player if sharing is enabled for that campaign.

## P1: polish if time remains

- Sweep obvious off-theme page colours back to the Mystic Tabletop palette.
- Check World Map, Chronicles/Notes, NPCs, Live Session, and Player Dashboard for red/orange/purple clashes.
- Hide or relabel unfinished controls instead of leaving buttons that do nothing.
- Keep handouts easy to find from both Campaign Prep and Live Play Mode.

## Do not add tonight

- New map engines.
- New AI modes.
- New OneDrive/import integrations.
- New large page redesigns that do not directly fix the play loop above.
